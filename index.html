How to play ?

  • Flip two cards to reveal them.
  • Match pairs of cards to keep them face-up.
  • Match all pairs to win the game!
  • Win in fewer moves to set a new high score!

Note: You can change difficulty mode while playing but that will restart the game. Default difficulty mode is easy.
